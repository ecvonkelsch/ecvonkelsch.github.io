Lemon poppy seed cake

  • serves 12
  • prep time 15 mins
  • cook time 35 mins
  • ready in 50 mins
  • 181/4 oz lemon cake mix
  • 31/2 oz instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 4 eggs
  • 3/4 c vegetable oil
  • 11/4 c water
  • 1/2 c poppy seeds
  • 21 oz lemon pie filling
  • 1 c milk
  • 8 oz whipped topping, thawed
  • PREHEAT oven to 350˚f. Grease and flour a 9" × 13" pan.
  • CSTIR together cake mix and vanilla pudding mix in a large bowl. Make a well in the center and pour in eggs, oil, water, and poppy seeds. Beat on low speed until blended. Beat for an additional 4 min on medium speed. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  • BAKE in the preheated oven for 30–40 min, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Allow to cool completely. Spread lemon pie filling over the cooled cake.
  • MIX instant lemon pudding with milk. Let stand about 5 mins until fairly set. Fold in whipped topping and spread over pie filling. Refrigerate at least 4 hrs before serving.
